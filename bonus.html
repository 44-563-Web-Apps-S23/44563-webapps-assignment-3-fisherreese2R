Firebrick Raven

The Firebrick Raven is based in the city of Los Angeles. This superhero main goal is to catch
bank robbers and art thiefs all across the city to keep the town of Los Angeles safe.
The only weakness the Firebrick raven is a large amount of red sun radiation which is only found
in the mythical mountains of Klipnifica.

Superpowers

  1. Power absorption
  2. Sonic Scream
  3. X-ray Vision
