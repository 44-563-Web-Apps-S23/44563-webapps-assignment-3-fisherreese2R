Ranger's Apprentice Series

This book (and series) is the best I have ever read. The characters are very relatable,
and are humorous. I would recommend this for anyone, even non-readers, as it had me hooked.
The Rangers Apprentice Series and Brotherband series are the best books I have ever read, and
I have read them all 3-4 times.
